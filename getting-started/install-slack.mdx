## Sign in to Pylon

1. Go to [Pylon](https://app.usepylon.com) (https://app.usepylon.com) and click
   `Continue with Slack`

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/slack-setup-1.png" />
</Frame>
2. Select the Slack Workspace that you want to add Pylon to

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/slack-setup-2.png" />
</Frame>
3. Click `Allow`

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/slack-setup-3.png" />
</Frame>

<Check>Your individual account is setup!</Check>

## Add Pylon to your Slack Workspace

<Tip>
  You'll need a Slack Admin to install the Pylon into your Slack Workspace.
</Tip>

1. After signing in, click the `Connect to Slack` Button

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/slack-setup-4.png" />
</Frame>
2. Select the Slack Workspace that you want to add Pylon to

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/slack-setup-2.png" />
</Frame>
3. Click `Allow`

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/slack-setup-5.png" />
</Frame>

<Check>Pylon has been added to your Slack!</Check>

## Additional Setup

### Set Bot Name

When Pylon is enabled in a customer channel, the customer will see that
`@Pylon was added to the channel`. You can rename the bot so that instead it
shows `@[YourCompany Support] was added to the channel` instead.

1. In Slack, click `Add Apps` in the sidebar

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rename-1.png" />
</Frame>
2. Find and click `Pylon`

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rename-2.png" />
</Frame>
3. Click the `About` tab

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rename-3.png" />
</Frame>
4. Click `Configuration`

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rename-4.png" />
</Frame>
5. Scroll down to the section called `Bot User` and click `Edit`

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rename-5.png" />
</Frame>
6. Enter the name of your company plus the work "Support" and hit `Save Changes`

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rename-6.png" />
</Frame>

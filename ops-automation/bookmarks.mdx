Automatically set bookmarks for Pylon enabled customer channels.

You can see bookmarks on the
[Workflows Page](https://app.usepylon.com/workflows).

<Info>
  You cannot modify the bookmarks in the UI yet (coming soon). To set a new
  bookmark, message the Pylon team in your shared channel the desired `URL`,
  `Icon`, and `Label`.
</Info>

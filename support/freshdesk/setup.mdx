<Note>You'll need a Freshdesk administrator to complete the setup.</Note>

1. Send the Pylon team your Freshdesk subdomain in your shared channel. You can
   find your subdomain in your Freshdesk URL
   `https://{subdomain}.freshdesk.com`.
2. [Find your API token](https://support.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions/articles/215517-how-to-find-your-api-key)
   in Freshdesk and send it over to the Pylon team
3. Click `Manage` on the Freshdesk app in the
   [Pylon Integrations Page](https://app.usepylon.com/integrations) and confirm
   the user mapping is set properly

All done!

<Check>You've setup the Freshdesk Integration</Check>

<Note>You'll need a Salesforce administrator to complete the setup.</Note>

1. Send the Pylon team your Salesforce subdomain in your shared channel. You can
   find your subdomain in your Salesforce URL
   `https://{subdomain}.salesforce.com`.
2. Visit the [Integrations Page](https://app.usepylon.com/integration) and hit
   `Connect` under Salesforce Service
3. Authorize the Salesforce Service App
4. Click `Manage` on the Salesforce Service App and confirm the user mapping is
   set properly

All done!

<Check>You've setup the Salesforce Service Integration</Check>

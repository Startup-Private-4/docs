title: "Additional Ticket Message"
icon: "message-plus"
---

You can post an additional message post in Slack when new messages are opened.

Common use cases include:

- Notifying customers that your company is at an offsite
- Special Holiday Hours (e.g. Winter Holiday)
- Asking for a free-form field (e.g. Please include your Model ID)

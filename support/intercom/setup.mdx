<Note>You'll need a Intercom administrator to complete the setup.</Note>

1. Visit the [Integrations Page](https://app.usepylon.com/integration) and hit
   `Connect` under Intercom
2. Authorize the Intercom App
3. Click `Manage` on the Intercom app and confirm the user mapping is set
   properly

All done!

<Check>You've setup the Intercom Integration</Check>

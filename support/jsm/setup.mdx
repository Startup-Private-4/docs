<Note>You'll need a Jira administrator to complete the setup.</Note>
1. Go to your Jira [company].atlassian.net/jira/marketplace/discover

2. Go to Apps > Manage your apps in the top bar

3. Click Settings, make sure “Enable private listings” is checked and click
   “Apply”

4. Refresh the Jira page

5. Click Upload app

6. Paste in the JSM App URL (ask the Pylon team for this) and click “Install”
